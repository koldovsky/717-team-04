Sansevieria Trifasciata (ST1)

Product code 4

$36,00

It is a perennial plant with rosettes of 6-8 leaves in length. The leaves are hard, sword-like, up to 1 m long, dark green with light transverse wavy stripes, most brightly colored in young leaves. There are whitish-green flowers, collected in a cluster, with a strong aroma. Sansevieria Trifasciata blooms in spring and autumn.

Care:
Sansevieria will not die in low light conditions but you should not constantly keep it in the back of the room. It needs a lot of light, including sunlight.
